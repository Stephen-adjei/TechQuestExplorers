Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

What coding programs do you offer?

We offer coding programs for various age groups and skill levels, including introductory courses for beginners and advanced courses for experienced coders. Our programs cover languages such as Python, JavaScript, and more.

How can I enroll in your coding programs?

Enrollment in our coding programs is easy! Simply visit our website and navigate to the enrollment section. You can choose your desired program, fill out the enrollment form, and make the necessary payments online.

What are the schedules for your coding classes?

We offer flexible scheduling options for our coding classes to accommodate different time zones and student preferences. You can choose from weekday or weekend classes, as well as morning or evening sessions, based on your availability.
